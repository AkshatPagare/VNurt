What's in it for Students

Various researches show that the employability quotient of engineering students in India is very low. Almost 83% of engineering graduates are unfit for employment.

VNURT believes that this situation is an opportunity for us to do something that can not only increase the employability of students, but also create a talent force that will make India a global powerhouse of technology. To do so, we have created a portal VNURT.com which will bring in industry, educational institutes, professionals and students in one place. This ecosystem will improve information sharing, provide opportunities to learn, leverage on each other’s experience. Through VNURT we also want to ensure that the physical distance from opportunities is not a roadblock for students and anyone from any part of country should get a chance to prove their worth.
